Valhalla Tabletop

    A web applcation built to track play throughs of different popular board games and show users a complete lists of the games they play while calculating statistics about their most played, most won, and other information. Each game record stores unique information from that game so the data can be further analysised for future more depth statistics for each player. Users are able to add friends and view their profiles to see what games they are playing.

    Along with tracking game information other tools to aid in playing board games were created and added onto the site to expand use: Dominion Kingdom Generator, Score Counter, and Card Viewer.

Add Game Records

    Record your play history with ease. Choose from a list of preselected games or use a custom template to log games not yet in the official database. This flexibility ensures you can track every session.

View Previous Records

    Easily browse your game records with robust filtering options. Sort by favorites, mechanics, dates, themes, and more to quickly find any session or analyze your habits over time.

Dominion Card Viewer

    Search and explore cards from Dominion, the acclaimed deck-building game. Use filters to browse by expansion, card type, or specific categories, making it simple to find the cards you need or discover new combinations.

Dominion Kingdom Generator

    Stop sifting through piles of cards! The Kingdom Generator lets you select the expansions you own and customize the card types for your game. Landscape cards can be included too. When you generate a kingdom, the relevant victory cards are automatically selected and sent into the Dominion Score Counter, streamlining setup and scoring for your session.

Dominion Score Counter
